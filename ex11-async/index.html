Be faster than your opponent!

Instructions

  • Click "Start Game" to start a round
  • Try to press the correspoding key when the arrow stops spinning
  • The fastest player will move his car forwards - the lose will not move
  • Get to the finish line before your opponent to win the game!

Commands

  • Player 1: type 'A' to run
  • Player 2: type 'L' to run
